The Origins of the PBS Logo

BY DAN PERRERA

This is fun and interesting video of Herb Lubalin talking about how the PBS logo came to be. The piece delves lightly into how clients and designers work together but oversimplifies that issue a bit in favor of time. However it’s great to see the different identities the designer and PBS considered before deciding on the final logo.
